# Global Unicorn Startup Analytics Dashboard (2007–2022)

https://datastudio.google.com/u/0/reporting/11e438ee-30a1-482b-8430-bb258a468e65/page/Vp31F

## Dashboard Overview

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the global unicorn startup ecosystem from 2007 to 2022. The dashboard is designed to help users explore startup growth trends, industry distribution, company valuations, investor activity, and geographic concentration of unicorn companies.

---

## Dashboard Components

### Executive KPIs

The top section provides a high-level summary of the unicorn landscape:

* Total Unicorns
* Total Unicorn Valuation ($B)
* Total Countries
* Total Industries

Users can also filter the dashboard by country and date range.

### Unicorn Creation by Year

This trend chart shows the number of unicorn companies created each year.

**Purpose:**

* Identify periods of rapid startup growth.
* Highlight changes in unicorn creation over time.
* Detect significant spikes in startup funding activity.

### Distribution of Unicorns by Industry

The donut chart visualizes how unicorn companies are distributed across industries.

**Purpose:**

* Understand which sectors dominate the unicorn ecosystem.
* Compare industry representation across the market.


### Unicorns by Country

The geographic map highlights countries based on their unicorn company presence.

**Purpose:**

* Identify global startup hubs.
* Compare regional concentration of unicorn companies.
* Visualize geographic distribution patterns.


### Industry Contribution to Total Valuation

The treemap displays the share of total unicorn valuation contributed by each industry.

**Purpose:**

* Compare industry valuation strength.
* Identify sectors creating the most enterprise value.
* Understand market concentration by industry.

### Most Investor-Backed Unicorns

This table ranks unicorn companies based on investor participation.

**Purpose:**

* Identify companies attracting the largest investor interest.
* Compare fundraising activity across unicorn companies.


### Top Unicorn Companies

This table highlights the highest-valued unicorn companies and includes:

* Company Name
* Country
* Industry
* Year Joined
* Total Valuation ($B)

**Purpose:**

* Identify market-leading unicorn companies.
* Compare valuations across industries and countries.

## Key Insights

* The United States dominates the global unicorn ecosystem by both company count and valuation.
* Fintech represents the largest unicorn industry by overall valuation.
* Unicorn creation accelerated significantly after 2018 and reached its peak in 2021.
* China and India have emerged as major global startup ecosystems.
* Unicorn activity remains concentrated within a relatively small number of countries and industries.


## Dashboard Objective

The primary goal of this dashboard is to provide a data-driven view of the global unicorn startup landscape and enable users to explore trends in startup growth, valuation, industry performance, and geographic distribution through an interactive reporting experience.
